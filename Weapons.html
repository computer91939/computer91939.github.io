Amygdalan Arm: This arm from a minor amygdala can be found in the Hunter's Nightmare.

Beast Claw: Resembling the claw of a malformed beast, once wielded by Irreverent Izzy. This weapon can be found in an Ailing Loran Chalice Dungeon.

Beasthunter Saif: A transformable, curved longsword once used by the old hunters. The Beasthunter Saif can be found in the Hunter's Nightmare.

Beast Cutter: An iron cleaver which can transform into heavy whip. Located in the Hunter's Nightmare.

Blade of Mercy: A single blade that can be transformed into two, the blade of mercy was adopted by the hunters of hunters. Purchasable from the messengers after killing Eileen the Crow.

Bloodletter: Wielded by Brador, this weapon is used to "cleanse ones blood of imupurities." Kill Brador to obtain this weapon.

Boom Hammer: As the name suggests the Boom Hammer is an exploding hammer. Located within the Hunter's Nightmare.

Burial Blade: Gerhman's weapon of choice. Purchasable from the messengers after killing Gehrman.

Chikage: A weapon of the royal guards of Cainhurst Castle. Purchasable from the messengers after joining the Cainhurst Vilebloods covenant.

Church Pick: An old weapon of the Healing Church, located in the Hunter's Nightmare.

Holy Moonlight Sword: A magically imbued sword discovered and wielded by Ludwig of the Healing Church. Obtainable after defeating Ludwig, The Holy Blade.

Hunter Axe: One of three trick weapons you can choose from at the beginninng of the game, this axe is able to be transformed into a larger axe with large sweeping attacks.

Kirkhammer: A hammer-sword hybrid, great for both quick and heavy attacks. Purchasable from the messengers after obtaining the Sword Hunter Badge.

Kos Parasite: This weapon is as the name implies, a wearable parasite from the Orphan of Kos. Obtainable after defeating the Orphan of Kos.

Logarius' Wheel: A giant wheel wielded by Martyr Logarius' executioners. Purchasable from the messengers upon gaining the Wheel Hunter Badge.

Ludwig's Holy Blade: Brandished by Ludwig and fellow Healing Church hunters this two in one sword offers both quick and heavy hitting attacks. Purchasable from the messengers after gaining the Sword Hunter Badge.

Rakuyo: Lady Maria's weapon of choice, comending great dexterity. Loacted at the bottom of a well in the Fishing Hamlet.

Reiterpallasch: A weapon of the Knights of Cainhurst that is both a rapier and pistol in one. Found in the entrance hall of Cainhurst Castle.

Rifle Spear: As the name suggests this weapon is a rifle-spear combo wielded by the Powder Keg hunters. Obtainable from within Old Yharnam.

Saw Cleaver: One of the three starting weapons available to the player at the start of the game. This weapon starts out as a saw, and once transformed becomes a long-distance cleaver.

Saw Spear: Very much like its counterpart, the Saw Cleaver, the Saw Spear transforms from saw to spear. Purchasable from the messengers upon acquiring the Saw Hunter Badge.

Simon's Bowblade: A weapon belonging to the hunter Simon, this curved blade can be transformed into a bow for long distance occasions.

Stake Driver: Another of the Powder Keg hunter's creations, this weapon charges up to drive stakes into it's prey. Purchasable from the messengers after acquiring the Powder Keg Hunter Badge.

Threaded Cane: One of the three starting weapons available to the player at the start of the game. The Threaded Cane is simply a cane transformable into a whip.

Tonitrus: Developed by Archibald of the Healing Church this mace deals lightning bolt damage to its enemies.

Whirligig Saw: The Powder Keg's finest creation wielded by Old Hunter Valtr. This mace-saw hybrid boasts true strength and speed. Located in the Hunter's Nightmare.
