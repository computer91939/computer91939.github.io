Abandoned Old Workshop: A workshop for Hunter's to hone in their skills and work on their weaponry. Located in Cathedral Ward.

Byrgenwerth: An old college for deciples of the old blood. Buried deep in the forbidden woods.

Cathedral Ward: A central location of Yharnam and a central location of the game itself. Home to the Healing Church.

Fishing Hamlet: Home to the Orphan of Kos this forgotten town was once the prey for many inhumane experiments.

Forbidden Woods: A woods the church forbids those to seek out as it is home to the banished and many secrets wished to be kept as such.

Forsaken Castle Cainhurst: The remains of an old and banished notability take refuge here after being hunted to the brink of extinction.

Hemwick Charnel Lane: Only sorrow and misery await those who seek out this path. Fear the Witches of Hemwick.

Hunter's Dream: The only safe place in this nightmare world... or so one would think. This is the hub where one can make purchases and upgrades.

Hunter's Nightmare: Home to many hunters, but do not be fooled for they are not your ally.

Hypogean Gaol: A prison where snatchers take you upon your defeat by their hand.

Iosefka's Clinic: Once a nurse to the sickly of Yharnam Iosefka now uses her clinic for "higher" modifications on her patients.

Lecture Building: Once a lecture hall to students of the blood.

Mergo's Loft: Home to Mergo the Wet Nurse.

Nightmare Frontier: Home to Amygdala. You will find yourself here as a result of trusting patches the spider as other hunters have.

Nightmare of Mensis: Home to Micolash, host of the nightmare. This area was created by Micolash while seeking audience with Mergo the Wet Nurse.

Reasearch Hall: This is no ordinary research hall. The research conducted here in secrecy by the Healing Church was used to transcend to greathood.

Upper Cathedral Ward: Home to the highest ranking officials of the healing church, the choir, and their most sought after secrets.

Yahar'Gul, Unseen Village: Home to what once was a place of worship and scholars. The city of Yahar'Gul has seen better days.

Yharnam: The central area of Bloodborne and home to the scourge of beasts. Yharnam branches out to all other locations and narratives of the game.
