Amygdala: One of the Great Ones. You can trigger a boss fight with Amygdala in the Nightmare Frontier.

Blood Starved Beast: An old beast sealed off in the depths of Old Yharnam.

Celestial Emissary: One of the Great Ones who remains hidden away by the Choir in Upper Cathedral Ward.

Cleric Beast: Once a cleric of the Healing Church turned beast by seeking the powers of the old Blood. The cleric Beast is typically the first boss you encounter upon awakening in Yahrnam.

Darkbeast Paarl: Located upon making your way through the Hypogean Gaol, Darkbeast Paarl is an optional boss.

Ebrietas, Daughter of the Cosmos: Yet another Great One Ebrietas is hidden away in Upper Cathedral Ward behind the Celestial Emissary.

Father Gascoigne: Seemingly guarding the Oedon Chapel Father Gascoigne was once a proud hunter now turned beast by the blood.

Gehrman, the First Hunter: Gerhman first appears to us as gentle guide throughout our time in the Hunter's Dream, however we discover his true identity by progressing the story.

Lady Maria of the Astral Clocktower: Lady Maria was once a member of the royal family of Cainhurst and disciple of Gehrman. Now she remains in the Astral Clocktower guarding the gruesome history of the Fishing Hamlet.

Laurence, the First Vicar: Laurence was once a scholar and student of Provost Willem, he set out to discover the secrets of the old blood only to succumb to its power. This fiery Vicar now sleeps in the Hunter's Nightmare.

Living Failures: A sickening result of the Healing Church's experiments, these "failures" were an attempt at man-made Great Ones.

Ludwig, the Holy Blade: Ludwig was another of Gerhman's disciples. He was a founding member of the Healing Church who like many others succumb to bloodlust. Ludwig now remains trapped within the Hunter's Nightmare.

Martyr Logarius: Martyr Logarius was once a high ranking member of the Healing Church. He now guards a vileblood secret hidden away in Cainhurst Castle.

Mergo's Wet Nurse: The wet nurse to the child of Mergo, Mergo's Wet Nurse stands guard over the source of the Nightmare of Mensis.

Micolash, Host of the Nightmare: A former student at Byrgenwerth, Micolash gave birth to the Nightmare of Mensis through his dreams and is now its host.

Moon Presence: Likely the most powerful Great One, Moon Presence remians in charge of all who call the Hunter's Dream home.

Orphan of Kos: An infant Great One whose mother was slain by hunters in the Fishing Hamlet.

Rom, the Vacuous Spider: Another scholar of Byrgenwerth who transcended humanity and now guards it from the true horrors around them.

Shadow of Yharnam: Three shadowy figures located deep within the Forbidden Woods who seem to guard the entrance to Byrgenwerth.

The One Reborn: Another rejected attempt of mankind's to create a Great One. Found within Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village.

Vicar Amelia: A disciple of Laurence and high ranking member of the Healing Church who like her mentor, fell victim to the old blood and now guards the password to the Forbidden Woods within the Grand Cathedral.

Witch of Hemwick: Residing within Hemwick the Witch of Hemwick awaits to collct ones eyes who wander into her abode, presumingly to make contact with the Great Ones.
