Beast Patient: A beast patient is a huntsman turned full beasthood. These creatures bide their time mainly within Old Yharnam and the hunter's Nightmare.

Bloodlicker: Resembling a flea these creatures feed off blood and store in it their engorged bellies. These abominations can be located outside Castle Cainhurst and within the Hunter's Nightmare.

Brain of Mensis: Located wihtin the Nightmare of Mensis this pile of rotting brain and eyes is known as a Great One.

Brainsucker: An alien-like monster that will grab you by the head an use its tentacle to drain your life. You will encounter these enemies in Cathedral Ward, Upper Cathedral Ward, Iosefka's Clinic, and Byrgenwerth.

Brick Troll: A large troll that will stomp you with the very brick it wields. Located around Central Yharnam, Cathedral Ward, the Healing Church Workshop, Hemwick Charnel Lane, and Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village.

Carrion Crow: Attracted to the scent of blood these crows have become immobilized from gorging themselves and now sprawl around helplessly looking for prey. These unsightly heathens are known to inhabit Central Yharnam, Cathedral Ward, Upper Cathedral Ward, Old Yharnam, Hemwick Charnel Lane, the Forbidden Woods, Iosefka's Clinic, and the Nightmare of Mensis.

Chapel Giant: Resembling humans these giants are guards of the church who seek to stop anyone from entering the Grand Cathedral. They are found bumbling around Cathedral Ward, the Forbidden Woods and the Lecture Building's second floor.

Church Servant: These humanoid beings come in multiple variataions. They wander around the cathedral grounds looking to punish intruders. Located in Cathedral Ward, Upper Cathedral Ward, and the Research Hall.

Cramped Casket: As the name states this grotesque pile of flesh can be found in hot pursuit of those who dare enter Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village.

Executioner: A giant executioner with a giant axe. Found hiding about Central Yharnam, Cathedral Ward, the Forbidden Woods, and Hemwick Charnel Lane.

Flourescent Flower: What can only be interpreted as life from a far away place, these bioluminescent centipedes spit lasers and fireballs attempting to protect the grounds of Byrgenwerth.

Garden of Eyes: Another "guardian" of the grounds of Byrgenwerth. These fly-like creatures have adorned a crown of eyeballs.

Giant Fishman: Hidden away in the Fishing Hamlet, these massive shark-like creatures will tear you to pieces.

Hemwick Grave Woman: The women of Hemwick are nothing to mess with as they have found themsleves immune to the scourge of beasts. Hemwick is not their only home as they also protect Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village.

Huntsman: Men slightly-turned beast, but men no more. This basic enemy will be found all over including, Central Yharnam, Cathedral Ward, the Healing Church Workshop, Hypogean Gaol, Hemwick Charnel Lane, the Forbidden Woods, Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village, and the Hunter's Nightmare.

Maneater Boar: Giant boars afflicted with the scourge of beasts and an appetite for men. These boars are found roaming Central Yharnam, Hypogean Gaol, the Forbidden Woods, and the Nightmare of Mensis.

Mergo's Attendant: Attendants of Mergo the Wet Nurse these small humanoids patrol the grounds of Mergo's Loft.

Mergo's Chief Attendant: Never far from their much smaller counterpart, these Chief Attendants pack a wallop. Located in Mergo's Loft.

Merciless Watcher: A large Pthumerian locked away in the depths of the Chalice Dungeons.

Nightmare Apostles: A horde of giant spiders within the Nighmare of Mensis.

Rabid Dog: Dogs that have fallen to the scourge of beasts and no longer have a care for human life. Located in Central Yahrnam, Cathedral Ward, the Forbidden Woods, Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village, the Nightmare of Mensis, and the Hunter's Nightmare.

Scourge Beast: A giant werewolf and the first enemy you will encounter in the game. Found in Iosefka's Clinic, Central Yharnam, Old Yharnam, Upper Cathedral Ward, and Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village.

Slime Scholars: Once human scholars of the blood, they are now slug-like beings overcome with an intense knowledge of the Great Ones. Can be found inhabiting the Lecture Building.

Snake Ball: A mass of snakes inhabiting the Forbidden Woods.

Snatcher: A kidanapper of sorts who will punish you for approaching it by bagging you up and transporting you to the Hypogean Gaol. Can be approached in Cathedral Ward, the Forbidden Woods, and Yahar'Gul the Unseen Village.

Vermin Host: Hunstmen afflicted with a parasitic snake. Found wandering aimlessly in the Forbidden Woods.

Winter Lantern: An EXTREMELY dangerous enemy to be avoided at all costs. A giant mass of eyes adornes a human figure. Stay vigilant for the lanterns wandering around the Nightmare Frontier, the Nightmare of Mensis, and the Fishing Hamlet.
